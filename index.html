PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is important to AR Physics Experiment creator Csaba Toth. This privacy statement provides information about any data the AR Physics Experiment would collect.

Personal information collection

AR Physics Experiment does not collect any kind of personal information or data whatsoever. Tuning the physics experiment paraameters save the settings locally on the device and it's not communicated anywhere.

Using information

The camera permission is needed because after application start the Augmented Reality engine (Sceneform) scans the surroundings to orientate in the 3D sorroundings and determine possible floor planes to place the tower.

On top of that constant camera video stream is necessary to provide the actual reality part of the augmented reality experience. AR Physics Experiment does not record, save or transfer any of the camera or any kind of sensor or other data whatsoever.

The app doesn't contain any advertisement framework and does not require any authentication either.

Securing data, transfers

Since no data is transferred, and the experiment settings are not sensitive data, securing is not applicable.

Updating this statement

AR Physics Experiment may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

Please check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

Other frameworks

AR Physics Experiment is not responsible for the privacy policies or data handling practices of any third party library used. If internals of Android operating system, Sceneform, ARCore, Physics simulation engines or any implicitly involved framework of the eco-system would record anything, that's outside of the jurisdiction of AR Physics Experiment application creator.
